Comprehensive Geoscience Solutions

Geoteclite offers geological and geophysical technical expertise across the entire lifecycle of large-scale energy projects. We have vast international experience having worked on many diverse and challenging projects, both marine and on land, varying from environments such as the thick jungle in Africa to adverse metocean conditions in the South Atlantic.

Our expertise in detecting geophysical & geological anomalies across a variety of datasets will prove invaluable in helping resolve the non-uniqueness of the subsurface model. This depth of experience provides a solid base for understanding the complexities inherent in most projects; from problematic seabed and ground conditions to heterogenous reservoir architecture and everything in between.

Geoteclite offers a fully integrated technical and commercial approach that can meet your needs for the energy transition.
