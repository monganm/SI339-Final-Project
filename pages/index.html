Welcome to GeoTecLite

Geoteclite aspires to become a fully integrated energy company that can offer technical and commercial expertise to the energy industry. Geoteclite is a newly formed limited company that has vast experience in the energy industry and is now utilizing these transferable skills to incorporate into the Renewable Energy sector. The company aspires to become a fully integrated energy company that can offer technical and commercial expertise to the energy industry. We are passionate about the energy Industry, and we would like to play a part in the just energy transition We can leverage our experiences in the oil and gas industry where we have worked the entire lifecycle of major international projects to help guide and support Renewable Energy projects in each phase of their development.

About Us

Geoteclite was founded by Joe Mongan in 2020 to support the just energy transition, leading the EU GeoEnergy-2 deep geothermal meta-cluster project market visits to Canada, Kenya, and Costa Rica on behalf of the Geoscience Ireland cluster. Prior to this, he spent 27 years at Tullow where he headed up the Geophysical Technology & Operations teams and represented Tullow on several industry collaborative groups, including a role as Chairman of the Society of Exploration Geophysicists (SEG) Global Affairs Committee in 2015. He sat on the iCRAG Governance Committee for Phase 1 and currently on the iCRAG Industry Advisory Committee. He was Executive Director of Tullow Oil Ltd from 2017-2020 prior to his current role with PIPCO, as Project Managing Director. The company has been providing technical project management and consultancy services in the global energy and mineral resources sectors for almost 25 years. PIPCO’s recent focus is on clean energy and transfer of skills from more traditional to cleaner and renewable resources. He holds B.Sc. Geology, Dipl.Sc. and M.Sc. Applied Science from University College Dublin.
